一.Using the Strategy pattern in algorithm design

1.街区距离和欧式距离

点A(x1, y1),点B(x2, y2),则
街区距离(cityblock distance)：
$$d=\left|x_2-x_1\right|+\left|y_2-y_1\right|$$
欧式距离(Euclidean distance )
SSd=\sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}$$
